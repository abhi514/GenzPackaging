Innovation ( 01 )

Our design teams have been creating packaging solutions for decades and our staff is well trained and intimately familiar with all technical elements of packaging products. We are always in the process of designing new solutions and building new moulds to bring those solutions to market.

Global Sourcing ( 02 )

Our procurement team is well versed in analyzing projects in order to ensure that the best manufacturing solutions are chosen. They work in tandem with our Quality Department to coordinate audits and certifications for our partner plants. Our purchasing team has ongoing projects with global plants which provides us flexibility and strengths to move projects and initiatives forward. Let us help support your procurement and purchasing requirements.

Inventory Control ( 03 )

Our inventory team specializes in inventory tracking, product quality care, and product storage optimization. We offer a customized inventory control service to the market that allows our clients to focus on manufacturing efficiencies and cost reductions. Our model provides for product availability to accommodate changing manufacturing schedules and cost stability so project budgets can be respected. Our inventory team works with our quality team for required AQL sampling, inspection services, or custom repacking/carton-labeling requirements. Our inventory team works with our logistics team to continuously expedite products into and out of our facilities to meet customer requirements. Let us bring you value by managing inventory of your components.

Decorating ( 04 )

GenzPackaging has been in the decorating business for more than 5 years. We bring a value added approach to decorating that allows our customer base to save time and costs by engaging a single vendor for a desired decorated product. Decorating services include silkscreen, hot stamping, label application, and shrink sleeve application. We decorate on all plastic and glass products. Let us provide you with a fully decorated product.

Consolidated Bottle coordinates global logistic services for import and export with all continents. Our inventory control services consolidate supply for multinational companies which are in-turn shipped to sister plants and contract manufacturers globally. Locally, we manage a dedicated trucking fleet that offers timely delivery within the Greater Toronto and Montreal areas. Let us manage your logistics responsibilities.
